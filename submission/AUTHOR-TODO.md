# Author actions required before clicking "submit" (cannot be automated)

These require your identity, accounts, or institutional authority. Everything else in this
package is complete and reproducible.

1. **Affiliation & identity.** Confirm the "University of Minnesota, Twin Cities"
   affiliation and your author name/email are exactly as you are entitled to use them, and
   that listing them is consistent with your institution's policy. If the affiliation is
   not correct, fix it in `paper/main.tex` `\address{...}` and rebuild.
2. **CMT/submission account.** Create/log in to the ICASSP 2027 paper submission system
   (Microsoft CMT or the system named in the final CfP), and register as author.
3. **Final author kit.** When the ICASSP 2027 author/paper kit is posted, re-verify the
   template version (`spconf.sty`/`IEEEbib.bst`), the exact Compliance-with-Ethical-Standards
   wording, and the AI-disclosure policy against `compliance-checklist.md` (items marked ⧗).
4. **AI-assistance disclosure.** Confirm the manuscript's disclosure wording matches the
   final ICASSP/IEEE requirement for the submission year.
5. **Copyright / IEEE eCF.** At acceptance, complete the IEEE electronic Copyright Form.
6. **Topic area & COI.** Select the topic area (Signal Processing Theory & Methods) and
   enter conflict-of-interest domains in the submission form (draft in
   `submission-form-draft.md`).
7. **Do not** rewrite git history to hide AI use or manufacture anonymity — ICASSP 2027 is
   single-anonymous and AI use is disclosed; both are compliant as-is.
8. **Final click.** Only you should perform the final upload/submit.
